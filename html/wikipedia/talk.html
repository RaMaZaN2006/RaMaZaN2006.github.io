Longest nails

Nail (anatomy) § Growth says

The longest female nails to ever exist measure a total of 601.9 cm.[1]

I don't have the source volume, but I see the following problems:

  1. "to ever exist" is unknowable. And this is certainly a fact about the past; even if it was current when published,
    that was five years ago. Changing to "known to ever have existed".
  2. 601.9 cm =19.75 feet, which is absurdly impossible for a fingernail. The text says "fingernails", plural, and "a total of", but these are easy to overlook in a quick read.
    That comes to an average length of 19¾ inches per fingernail, = 1ʹ 7¾ʺ, which is astonishing but credible. Editing accordingly.
